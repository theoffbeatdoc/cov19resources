Contact Details of District Co-ordination Centre

District CoVID Co-ordination Centre (DCCC) has been established in the offices of District Magistrates in the Districts and in KMC for KMC area. DCCCs are operational 24x7. DCCCs are co-ordinating different activities and facilities like admission to dedicated safe Homes and CoVID Hospitals, availability of ambulances and hearse vans, supply of oxygen to hospitals and other infrastructure and equipments required for CoVID management in close collaboration with the office of CMOH and local bodies. District CoVID Co-ordination Centre can be reached by clicking green coloured tab next to tap to call under different districts / KMC.
