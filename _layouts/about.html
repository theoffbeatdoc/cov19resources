About Us

We are a group of dedicated individuals trying to gather and present available authentic data regarding the treatment and management of COVID-19 management in India. This is an undertaking to ensure only authentic and verified data gets disseminated among the people in need. THIS IS COMPLETELY A NON-PROFIT ENDEAVOUR.
