Useful International & Indian Government Resources

Check out these sites for authentic info and resources on COVID-19

WHO COVID-19 Dashboard

WHO presents official daily counts of COVID-19 cases and deaths worldwide, along with vaccination rates and other vaccination data, while providing a hub to other resources.

UNICEF COVID-19 Information Centre

The latest COVID-19 news and resources, as well as tips to protect you and your family.

Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India. The site provides extensive statistics and resources related to COVID-19.

National Health Portal of India

The National Health Portal aims to establish a single point access for authenticated health information for citizens, students, healthcare professionals and researchers.
