Things to note before you contribute info...

Here are some Do's and Don'ts that you need to know before your start contributing to our database.

You should do

  • Enter only those info that you have collected from trusted sources.
  • Check if the info already exists before you start putting in your info.
  • Be very careful while entering the info so that typos do not creep in.
  • Reach out to us if you need to update any info previously entered.

You should NOT

  • Enter info already existing in our data pages
  • Enter info from random sources with no authenticity at all.
  • Spam same info repeatedly.
  • Support any sort of unscrupulous businessmen by promoting their stuff.
